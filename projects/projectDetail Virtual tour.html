Niamad virtual tour

Cross-device UI/UX design project

Overview

As the real estate industry evolves alongside technological advancements, the traditional house viewing process is no longer sufficient to meet the changing needs of modern renters and buyers. To address this gap, this project aims to redesign the online house viewing and appointment booking experience — making it more intuitive, accessible, and flexible for users.
