Overview

One sentence summary for overview

Atlassian is renowned for its outstanding collaboration tools and project management software, providing efficient and innovative business solutions and earning accolades from many professionals. However, our products have limited use among students. To familiarize our users with our products early on and facilitate a smoother transition from student to workforce, we are currently focused on designing a new web-based application that will help student teams collaborate effectively and align our products with the workplace. Our mission is to make work more simple and more meaningful, enabling better connections and growth.
